Mr. Tambourine Man

From Bringing It All Back Home (1965)

Key: D Major Tempo: 84 BPM Difficulty: Intermediate Capo: None

Featured Performance

Newport Folk Festival, 1964

The Story Behind the Song

Written in February 1964 after an all-night trip from New Orleans, the surrealist imagery marked Dylan's move away from protest songs toward more personal, poetic writing.

The Byrds' electric cover in 1965 launched the folk-rock genre, reaching #1 on the Billboard charts. Their jangly 12-string guitar sound influenced countless bands.
