Early 60s Folk (1962-1963)

Blowin' in the Wind

The Freewheelin' Bob Dylan (1963)

Key: G major | Capo: None | Difficulty: Beginner

Chords: G C D

Progression: G - C - G | G - C - D | G - C - G - D - G

The song that established Dylan as the voice of a generation. Simple three-chord structure makes it perfect for beginners.

A masterclass in fingerpicking. The intricate Travis picking pattern and subtle chord variations make this a rewarding challenge.

Masters of War

Chords: Dm C Bb Am

Dylan's most direct protest song, a withering condemnation of war profiteers. The minor key intensifies the anger.

Electric Period (1965-1966)

Like a Rolling Stone

Key: C major | Capo: None | Difficulty: Intermediate

Chords: C Dm Em F G

Driving rock rhythm, organ-heavy arrangement

Often called the greatest rock song ever recorded. The descending chord progression and relentless snare drive changed popular music forever.

70s Masterpieces (1973-1976)
