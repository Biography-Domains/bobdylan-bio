Newport Folk Festival 1965

Dylan's electric performance of "Maggie's Farm" at Newport is one of the most legendary moments in music history. The crowd famously booed as Dylan "went electric," though whether they booed the sound quality or the betrayal of folk purism is still debated.

35 years later, RATM would take Dylan's rebellion even further, turning folk protest into metal fury.
