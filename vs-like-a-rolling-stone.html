Greatest Song of All Time

In 2004 and again in 2021, Rolling Stone magazine ranked "Like a Rolling Stone" as the greatest song of all time. The song fundamentally changed what rock music could be - proving that songs longer than 3 minutes could become hit singles and that rock could address serious, complex themes.

Fun fact: Rolling Stone magazine named themselves after this song (and the Muddy Waters song that inspired Dylan).
