ANTI-WAR ANTHEM

Masters of War

From The Freewheelin' Bob Dylan (1963)

Key: D Minor Tempo: 96 BPM Difficulty: Intermediate Capo: None

Featured Performance

Live Performance

The Story Behind the Song

Dylan's most direct protest song, a withering condemnation of war profiteers and weapons manufacturers. The melody is adapted from the English folk song "Nottamun Town."

The song's final verse, wishing death upon the masters of war, remains controversial. Dylan has performed it during every major American military conflict since Vietnam.
